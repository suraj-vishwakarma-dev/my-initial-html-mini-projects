Chocolate Cake

This is simple and delicious chocolate cake recipe you can bake at home.

Ingrediesnts

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup mil
  • butter

Steps

  1. preheat the oven 180degree celsius
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa, and baking powder.
  3. pour batter
  4. bake for 15 min
  5. let it cool
  6. Enjoy it!
